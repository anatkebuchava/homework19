MY APPROACH

5 Step problem solving process

On Empathy Phase, I research my users and use 5 why’s technique to identify root cause of a problem I’m trying to solve and gain an empathic understanding of it.

On Definition Phase I put together the research I’ve created and gathered during empathy phase. I analyze my observations and identify a problem and user to focus on.

On Ideation Phase, I’m ready to start generating ideas. I use I like, I wish, what if method for brainstorming, prioritize my ideas on a matrix, creating user stories and storyboard.

I start Prototyping Phase, by making user flows and sketching number of paper prototypes, based on ideas generated during the previous phase. After, I create digital wireframes and I’m getting ready to test it.

On the final phase of the design thinking process, I start making usability testing plan, and testing MVP of the product with target users. I learn my mistakes and start iterating them.

After testing, I iterate my design and make hi-fidelity prototype, which I test again. My mantra for design is: Test it-Iterate/Redesign it-Test it again!
